# Final Packet Prompt

Create an executable handoff packet from the AccumulatedArtifact, MergePacket, and LoopDecision.
Keep deferred work out of scope.

Include Goal, Current State, Scope, Out of Scope, Files To Inspect, Expected Changes, Environment Variables, Secret Handling, Stub Fallback, Verification, Failure Handling, Report Format, Risks, and User Decision Points.
Keep user decision points to three or fewer.
